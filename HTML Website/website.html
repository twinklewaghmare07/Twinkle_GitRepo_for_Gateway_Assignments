Nykaa is an Indian retail seller of beauty, wellness, and fashion products.

Falguni Nayar, a former managing director at Kotak Mahindra Capital Company, founded Nykaa in 2012. It was launched as an ecommerce portal curating a range of beauty and wellness products. The brand name Nykaa is derived from the Sanskrit word nayaka, meaning ‘one in the spotlight’.

In 2015, the company expanded from online-only to an omnichannel model and began selling fashion products. Nykaa hosts the ‘Nykaa Femina Beauty Awards’ in partnership with women’s lifestyle magazine Femina (India) since 2015. In 2019, Nykaa Fashion launched ‘The Power List’ in partnership with Vogue India. In October 2019, it partnered with Bollywood actress Katrina Kaif to launch the brand Kay Beauty.
